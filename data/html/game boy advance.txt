This is a '''list of games released for the [[Game Boy Advance]]''' handheld [[video game]] system. The number of games in this list is '''{{table row counter|tableno=3|ignore=1}}''', organized alphabetically by the games' localized English titles, or, when Japan-exclusive, their ''[[rōmaji]]'' transliterations.

Every GBA game features the disclaimers "Only for Game Boy Advance" and "Not compatible with other Game Boy systems".

The Game Boy Advance is a handheld video game system developed by [[Nintendo]] and released during the [[History of video game consoles (sixth generation)|sixth generation of video games]].

The final game released for the Game Boy Advance was the North American localization of ''[[Samurai Deeper Kyo]]'', which released as a bundle with a [[DVD]] set on February 12, 2008.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.imusic.dk/gboy/5707409000925/krea-2007-pixeline-i-pixieland-gameboy-gameboy|title=Pixeline I Pixieland Gameboy - Krea (GBOY) (2007) - imusic.dk|website=www.imusic.dk|language=da|access-date=2018-08-21}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.siliconera.com/2008/02/21/samurai-deeper-kyo-the-gba%E2%80%99s-swan-song/|title=Samurai Deeper Kyo, the GBA's swan song? - Siliconera|date=2008-02-21|work=Siliconera|access-date=2018-08-21|language=en-US}}</ref>
